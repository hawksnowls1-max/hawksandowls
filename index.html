🦉 WHO WE ARE

Hawks & Owls is an indie game studio built on curiosity, creativity, and a deep love for games. We're a small team that believes great games don't need giant budgets — just great ideas and the drive to see them through.


From real-time multiplayer puzzle battles to Elden Ring fan trivia, we build games that are fun to pick up and hard to put down. Everything runs on Android, and we're just getting started.

🎮 WHAT WE BUILD

Our games span puzzle, trivia, and multiplayer — always mobile-first, always free or fair to play. We use Unity as our core engine and build with tools the community loves.

UNITY PHOTON PUN 2 UNITY ADS ANDROID C# GOOGLE PLAY

📜 OUR MISSION

Build games that bring people together — whether that's competing in a puzzle clash or bonding over Elden Ring lore. We share our journey openly through dev logs, community posts, and gameplay videos so others can learn alongside us.

⚖️ PRIVACY & LEGAL

We take privacy seriously. Our games collect minimal data and are transparent about third-party services like Unity Ads and Photon. Read our full privacy policy on our official Google Site.
